Content Visibility & Access Control
🌐 Open to any app user
  • Team profile & identity
  • Team schedule & game results
  • League & tournament profiles
  • Celebrate feed
  • Public athlete profiles (opt-in)
Any user can search for and discover any team, league, tournament, or athlete. Celebrate is accessible without membership.
⚙️ Team-controlled

Each team sets their own visibility level:

Stats visibility
  • Any app user
  • Confirmed members only
  • Family & player (own stats only)
Livestream & replays
  • Open to any app user
  • Restricted to members only
👥 Members only
  • Team group chat
  • Roster details
  • RSVP & event management
Requires confirmed membership — not just following or account creation.
User Relationship Types
League / Tournament Admin
Creates and manages a league or tournament. Invites teams, sets schedules, can score any game.
Full org access
Staff / Coach
Manages the team. Creates schedule, adds roster, controls team privacy settings.
Full team access
Community Member
Parents, family, and fans confirmed as part of the team. Access to chat, RSVP, and member-restricted content.
Members-only access
Follower
Any user who follows a team. Public content from that team surfaces in their Celebrate feed. No membership required.
Public content only
Athlete / Parent ELEVATED
Has an athlete profile linked to roster entries across teams. Player-first view of clips, stats, and moments.
