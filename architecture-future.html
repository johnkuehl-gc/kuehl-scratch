Content Visibility & Access Control
🌐 Open to any app user
  • Team profile & identity
  • Team schedule & game results
  • Celebrate feed
  • Public athlete profiles (opt-in)
Any user can search for and discover any team or athlete. Explore is accessible without team membership.
⚙️ Team-controlled

Each team sets their own visibility level:

Stats visibility
  • Any app user
  • Confirmed members only
  • Family & player (own stats only)
Livestream & replays
  • Open to any app user
  • Restricted to members only
👥 Members only
  • Team group chat
  • Roster details
  • RSVP & event management
Requires confirmed membership — not just following or account creation.
User–Team Relationship Types
Staff / Coach
Manages the team. Creates schedule, adds roster, controls team privacy settings.
Full team access
Community Member
Parents, family, and fans confirmed as part of the team. Access to chat, RSVP, and member-restricted content.
Members-only access
Follower
Any user who follows a team. Public content from that team surfaces in their Explore feed. No membership required.
Public content only
