Athlete Profile is a separate, parent-managed experience that spans across teams

🌟
Athlete Profile
A parent-created, cross-team hub for one real child. Because a player's roster records across different teams are not linked in the system, this feature provides a centralized identity — surfacing stats, videos, and teams in one place.

Each real athlete can have one profile, manually connected to their roster entries across teams and sports.
